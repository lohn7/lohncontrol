About Lohn Economy

Lohn is a widely spread contractual form in various industrial fields of international cooperation relations, especially in the industry of clothing, footwear, furniture, machine building industry, electronics and appliance industry, food industry and petrochemical industry.

The lohn system consists of an economic business, carried out on a contractual basis, between two companies of different countries where one performs, for a retribution in cash or in kind, a custom-made product according to the designs, drawings and instructions given, usually with materials provided from the other party, which keep their right to trade that product under its own trademark.

The low cost of labor of the emerging economies makes these markets very attractive for the production of consumer goods.
The main benefit of producing goods in these markets is the exceedingly low production prices which maximizes profits.
The lohn production economy is a commercial activity that has been constantly developing since the 1960 and will undoubtedly withstand the test of time as there will always be a difference in the cost of labor of developing countries compared to the developed ones.

Why do I need blockchain and smart contracts? icon

Blockchain and smart contracts will allow us to minimize operational costs, financial inefficiencies and manual administration, bad trade execution or conflict resolution.

Smart contract technologies are capable of eliminating these frictions and therefore have potential to eliminate associated cost overkill.
