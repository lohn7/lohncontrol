Ask Me!

What is lohn ? icon

The processing in lohn system consists of an international economic business carried out on a contractual basis between two companies of different countries where one performs, for a retribution in cash or in kind, a custom-made product according to the designs, drawings, usually with raw materials and materials of the other companies, which keep their right to trade that product under its own trademark.

Where does Lohn production fit in the global economy? icon

At present, the annual production achieved in lohn at the worldwide level exceeds 10,000 B $.

Is the processing in lohn a new trend in the world economy ? icon

No, the concept is 60 years old but LOHN is looking to reinvent the practice by enhancing the processes through a trustless system.

How LOHN works ? icon

LOHN Token is an ERC20 digital cryptographic utility token issued by Lohn ecosystem, using blockchain technology that gives access to our tokenholders to benefit by profit of trading platforms of lohncontract.com.

What is your target ? icon

Our target is to empower businesses spread all over the world by connecting them through a secure network of factories and suppliers.

What are the functions of your platforms? icon

The main function of the LOHN ecosystem is to provide a convenient and secure environment for factories. The lohncontrol platform is where global factories interact with each other through the blockchain, with the help of smart contracts.

How is LOHN different from other blockchain projects of manufacturing ? icon

LOHN is not only about manufacturing industries but is designed to manage industrial production processes in any industrial branch.

What is the connection between lohncontrol.com and lohncontract.com ? icon

The lohncontrol.com project developed in the ethereum blockchain and is designed to efficiently manage trading processes on the regional platforms of lohncontract.com.
The mechanisms managed by the blockchain technology of the lohncontrol.com project have a major role to give mutual trust to all the parties involved in a lohn contract.

What problems is LOHN solving ? icon

Trust problems between the parties of the lohn contract and the traceability of the product. LOHN will also help to eradicate the practice of work without legal employment forms or exploitation of vulnerable people and children in emerging economies.

What is your model based on? icon

We are using a range of like:
1. Ethereum platform for smart contracts.
2. An innovative solution for higher transaction speed recording and the low costs of transactions.
3. IPFS databases for data recording and storage.

Does LOHN operate globally? icon

Yes, the platform will be available to clients around the world.
At the moment we are already operating our first platform in Eastern Europe (bursadelohn.ro). It is better than an MVP for now because is completely functionable.

Why do I need blockchain and smart contracts? icon

Blockchain and smart contracts will allow us to minimize operational costs, financial inefficiencies and manual administration, bad trade execution or conflict resolution.
These technologies allow to create platforms and ecosystems with built in trust characteristics which, in traditional economy, is typically achieved by using laborious and inefficient processes for document exchange, recording, auditing, bookkeeping and reconciliation. Smart contract technologies are capable of eliminating these frictions and therefore have potential to eliminate associated cost overkill.

What means "WE NOT TOUCH THE MONEY" philosophy ? icon

Settlement of payments between parties is done without our intervention.
Bank operations are running now through escrow accounts. We also look for the latest payment solutions developed in the blockchain that can be integrate it into the smart lohncontrol contract. There are many regional and global payment solutions that are suitable with our vision of developing partnerships.

How do you plan to develop your product? icon

We will develop the LOHN project in accordance with our roadmap. The team members are people with skills that cover the various sections of the project such as logistics, legal, product quality control, payment process.

What is bursadelohn.ro ? icon

It's a regional hub for the processing economy. It's our working platform also our MVP (minimum viable product) and is the first operational trading platform of lohncontract.com.

What is LOHN FOUNDATION ? icon

LOHN FOUNDATION is the founder's dream for building a creative sharing economy network for the benefit of all lohn contract parties. LOHN FOUNDATION aims to actively support the development of professional education throughout the world, to provide training to specialists from emerging economies, to contribute to the harmonization of international legislation on processing economy in lohn. It aims to help local authorities in disadvantaged areas of the world by supporting initiatives aimed at creating jobs for local communities. LOHN FOUNDATION aims to establish traceability of the labor force employed in emerging economies to prevent human exploitation by unregistered tax work, through excessive work or child involvement in industrial goods manufacturing processes.
